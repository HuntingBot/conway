This is a multiplayer version of Conway's Game of Life.

Players compete for space on the board. The leaderboard shows the players that occupied the most cells on the board at one time.

When an empty cell comes to life because it is a neighbor to three cells of multiple players, the new cell is assigned to the player occupying two of the three surrounding cells.
